Generate accessible color

It’s important to follow WCAG contrast guidelines to ensure your product is accessible. It’s difficult to maintain the aesthetic appearance of color while transforming it to meet certain contrast ratios. By interpolating in various color spaces, as well as incorporating a tint or shade, you can control how your color changes as it darkens and lightens so that the aesthetic identity is maintained.
